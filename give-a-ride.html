Give a Ride!

Give back to your community by helping seniors get safely from point A to point B! Modern means of paid rides can be scary nowadays, so Senior Ride Nashville was created to offer a safe alternative to you elderly neighbrs, friends, and family in the Nashville area. We are looking for eligable drivers to take seniors to appointments, get groceries, go to community centers, and more. Only a few hours of your time will help make a difference.

Eligible volunteers will:

  • have access to a vehicle to transport seniors to their destination;
  • be 21 or older;
  • complete an application and other required forms;
  • pass a criminal background and driving record check;
  • attend a training;
  • have a valid driverâ€™s license and vehicle liability insurance that meets Tennessee requirements (SRN recommends 100/300/100);
  • and enjoy spending time with our older adult neighbors.
