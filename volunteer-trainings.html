Volunteer Training!

Thank you for considering volunteering at Senior Ride Nashville! This is one of the best platforms to support and give back to the elderly in your own community.

We have traning sessions every week:

  • Monday through Thursday: 6pm - 9pm
  • Friday: 5pm - 8pm
  • Saturday: 12pm - 3pm, and 4pm - 7pm

You are eligible to give rides if:

  • have access to a vehicle to transport seniors to their destination;
  • be 21 or older;
  • complete an application and other required forms;
  • pass a criminal background and driving record check;
  • attend a training;
  • have a valid driverâ€™s license and vehicle liability insurance that meets Tennessee requirements (SRN recommends 100/300/100);
  • and enjoy spending time with our older adult neighbors.
